A little about me

Designing interfaces that people actually enjoy using.

I'm Marc, a frontend developer and Information Technology student from Angeles City. I specialize in building responsive and accessible web interfaces that balance visual design with practical functionality.

My primary focus is frontend development, where I enjoy transforming concepts into engaging user experiences. Alongside this, I continue developing my knowledge of PHP, Angular, and Vue.js to gain a deeper understanding of full web application workflows and backend integration.

I am also passionate about UI/UX and digital design, often contributing to the visual direction of projects through interface design, user-centered thinking, and creative problem-solving. Whether refining user journeys, designing layouts, or developing visual assets, I value the role of design in creating experiences that are both functional and memorable.
